My Caribou results


The Caribou Mathematics Competition is an annual online math competition designed for students from grades 3 to 12. It was created in 2012 by mathematician and educator, Henry Wolkowicz, to promote and encourage the learning and enjoyment of mathematics among students. The competition has grown in popularity over the years and is now offered in multiple countries around the world.

The competition consists of a series of six monthly contests, with each contest consisting of 5 to 10 problems that range in difficulty from relatively easy to very challenging. Students can participate in the contest from anywhere in the world, as long as they have access to the internet. The contest is designed to be accessible to students of all levels of mathematical ability, and there are separate divisions for students in grades 3-4, 5-6, 7-8, 9-10, and 11-12.
